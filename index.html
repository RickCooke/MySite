Experience

  • The Boeing Company

    Mobility Software Developer and Support Team Member

    During my most recent internship I worked with Boeing's Mobility IT group developing mobile technologies to increase efficiency in a manufacturing environment. This role allowed me to develop new applications for iOS, Android and other mobile platforms as well as support existing products. I was also able to contribute to use cases of future technologies such as wearable computing (i.e. Google Glass, Kopin Golden-i, etc.). Much of this experience also came with the opportunity to network with customers both within and outside of Boeing. This included meeting with customers to develop specifications for new applications that would be used throughout the enterprise.

    This internship culminated when I was presented with the opportunity to organize a regional technology fair for Boeing Southern California. Using the network I had developed working with various development teams I was able to recruit business teams from across the industry to showcase their product. Through my efforts this event showcased more technologies than ever before and had much higher attendance than any technology fair prior.
